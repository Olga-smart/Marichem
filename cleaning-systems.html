Cleaning Systems

Cleaning cargoes could be tough. Or you have the right equipment

Navijet C500E – 500 bar

NAVIJET C500E is an electric driven high pressure cleaning machine, operating with cold water (feed water temperature up-to max. 60 0C). This heavy duty HPC machine is ideal for paint and rust removal, as well as for any cleaning task on board.

NAVIJET is constructed of stainless steel frame, with sst cover, and pump is driven by low RPM motor for long time-life.
